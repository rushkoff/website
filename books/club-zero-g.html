Club Zero-G

Buy the Book ← All Books
FictionYoung AdultSci-Fi iBooks

A young adult novel set aboard a space station where teenagers discover that the adults' plan for humanity's future is not quite what they've been told.

Combining the energy of rave culture with the weightlessness of zero gravity, Club Zero-G is a fast-paced adventure story about teenagers finding their own path in a world where the grown-ups have lost their way.

Written for younger readers but with the same cultural intelligence as Rushkoff's non-fiction work, the book explores themes of authority, authenticity, and the difference between the future adults plan for us and the future we might build for ourselves.
