Documentaries & Media

Films

PBS Frontline documentaries and other media work examining technology, culture, and identity.

PBS Frontline

Generation Like

How social media has transformed the way teenagers interact with popular culture — and with each other. Rushkoff explores whether digital platforms empower young people or simply give marketing a new and more insidious face. A follow-up to The Merchants of Cool.

Watch on PBS Frontline

PBS Frontline

The Persuaders

An investigation into the ever-more-sophisticated world of persuasion and marketing. Rushkoff goes behind the scenes with top advertising professionals, political strategists, and social scientists to examine how they are reaching deep into our daily lives in an attempt to influence our thoughts and behavior.

Watch on PBS Frontline

PBS Frontline

Merchants of Cool

A report on the creators and marketers of popular culture for teenagers. Rushkoff examines how major corporations study, mimic, and manipulate youth culture to sell products back to teens — and how young people are beginning to push back.

Watch on PBS Frontline

Documentary

Present Shock

Examining contemporary temporal experience — the condition of living entirely in the present moment at the expense of narrative and context. Contrasting modern immediacy with traditional rhythms of attention and intention.
